Laurence-Olivier M. Foisy

Data Scientist

Doctoral student in Political Science at Université Laval, working on the development of reliable and valid methods for using large language models in political science research. My work examines how AI tools can be rigorously integrated into political inquiry, from measurement and inference to knowledge discovery.
